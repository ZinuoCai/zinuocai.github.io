# Zinuo Cai (蔡子诺)

Zinuo Cai is a Ph.D. Candidate in the School of Computer Science at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. He is a member of [AISIG Group](http://aisig.sjtu.edu.cn/), where he conducts his research under the guidance of his advisor, [Prof. Ruhui Ma](https://www.cs.sjtu.edu.cn/jiaoshiml/maruhui.html).

His research interests span three main areas:

* **Serverless Computing**: Works include AARC (DAC'25), Ephemera (TACO'25), SMORE (TPDS'25), FasDL (TC'24), and SMSS (JSAC'23).  
* **Large Model Inference**: Hermes (ICCD'25).  
* **Quantum Computing**: Works include QFI-Opt (SPE'25), HWDSQP (JSAC'25), and FedCompass (ICASSP'26).  

He received his Bachelor's degree from the School of Software at Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2021 and is currently pursuing his Ph.D., expected to graduate in March 2027.
