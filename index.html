About Me

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in the School of Computer Science at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, advised by Prof. Ruhui Ma at the AISIG Group. I received my B.S. degree from the School of Software at Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2021, and I expect to graduate with my Ph.D. in March 2027.

My research spans three main areas:

  • Serverless Computing: AARC (DAC’25), Ephemera (TACO’25), SMORE (TPDS’25), FasDL (TC’24), SMSS (JSAC’23).

  • Large Model Inference: Hermes (ICCD’24).

  • Quantum Computing: FedCompass (ICASSP’26), HWDSQP (JSAC’25), QFI-Opt (SPE’25).
